## Instructions for Use

1. **Open PlayerID App** and navigate to "Validate" tab
2. **Single Import**: Copy any URL above and paste into "Jersey Photo URL" field
3. **Batch Import**: Click "Batch Import" → "URLs" tab → paste multiple URLs (one per line)
4. **Validate**: For each imported photo, confirm or correct the jersey number
5. **Set Conditions**: Choose lighting (bright/normal/dark), distance (close/medium/far), angle
6. **Save**: Click "✅ Validate & Save" to add to training dataset

## Tips for Validation

- **Quality Check**: Skip blurry or unclear photos
- **Number Visibility**: Ensure jersey number is clearly visible
- **Diverse Conditions**: Mix of lighting and angles for robust training
- **Batch Processing**: Use batch import for efficiency
- **Target**: Aim for 50+ photos per jersey number for best results
